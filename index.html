About

Christophe experiments with space & immersion in order to facilitate telling stories and remote work. He also leads immersive projects to ensure that the artistic vision gets translated into the spatial medium and manages the technological pipeline. He founded his company CMCVR in 2017 and also gives lectures at the HSLU D&K.
